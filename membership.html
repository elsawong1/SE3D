Membership

Join SEƐD Today!

SEƐD welcomes individuals from all backgrounds who share a passion for STEAM, 3D printing, and design. There are no specific qualifications or prerequisites to join—just a willingness to learn and grow with us. Membership lasts for a one-semester term, and there are no mandatory participation requirements. We understand the demands of college life, so you're free to engage as your schedule allows. Become part of our community and start your SEƐD experience today!

How to Join:

  1. Complete the membership form (available in our Discord server under “resources”).
  2. Submit the $10 club fee:
    • Venmo: @uhmse3d, including the following details in the payment description: “SE3D, First Last”
    • Cash: Hand in person to an officer.
  3. Once your payment and membership form are processed, you will receive a confirmation email, signifying your official membership with SEƐD for the semester.
